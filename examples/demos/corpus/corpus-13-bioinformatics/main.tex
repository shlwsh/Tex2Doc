\begin{abstract}
We present a comprehensive analysis of regulatory elements across the human genome,
integrating chromatin accessibility, histone modifications, and transcription factor
binding data from multiple disease cohorts.
\end{abstract}

\section{Results}

\subsection{Chromatin Accessibility}

We identified \num{250000} accessible chromatin regions using ATAC-seq.
These regions were annotated to potential regulatory elements.
