\subsection{Classical NR-IQA Baselines (BRISQUE / NIQE-style)}
\label{sec:nr-iqa-baselines}

To isolate routing from the Edge-IQA feature design, we add \textbf{Bbrisque} (OpenCV BRISQUE on tongue ROI) and \textbf{Bniqe} (NSS Mahalanobis distance, NIQE-style) under the \emph{same} LangGraph resampling policy and retry budget as B2.
Validation medians set $\tau$ independently per scorer (Table~\ref{tab:nr-iqa}); edge latency means follow Table~\ref{tab:iqa-compare} ($\approx$85\,ms / 150\,ms vs.\ Edge-IQA $\approx$9\,ms).
