\subsection{COCO Tongue ROI Cropping}
\label{sec:roi}

ShezhenV3-COCO provides COCO boxes $[x,y,w,h]$; we union multi-instance annotations, pad 8\%, and crop before resize (\texttt{edge\_iqa/coco\_roi.py}).
Full-frame scoring includes cheeks and background Laplacian energy unrelated to tongue focus; ROI aligns the quality signal with downstream TCM classifiers.

\caption{Edge-IQA calibration: full frame vs.\ tongue ROI (val, $n{=}572$ per cohort).}

\hline

\end{tabular}
\end{table}

Table~\ref{tab:roi-ablation} compares calibration settings.
Minimum-clear minus maximum-blur separation remains negative in both settings, so $\tau_{B2}{=}0.465$ is a cohort operating point---we report routing outcomes and Spearman $\rho$ (M5, $\rho{\approx}0.36$--$0.47$) rather than perfect separability.
When COCO boxes are unavailable at runtime, Edge-IQA falls back to full-frame scoring (\texttt{use\_roi=false}).
