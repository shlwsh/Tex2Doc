\subsection{Ablation}
\label{sec:ablation}

The Supplementary Material sweeps routing threshold $\tau$ for B2 (500 frames, seed~0).
The deployed main matrix uses $\tau_{B2}{=}0.465$ from ROI validation; $\tau_{\mathrm{full}}{=}0.498$ appears in the full-frame ROI ablation (Table~\ref{tab:roi-ablation}).

Table~\ref{tab:algo-ablation} compares fixed-threshold B2 against dynamic $\tau_t$ (B2d), multi-frame EMA (B2m), and utility routing (B2u) on 60 test frames (seed~0).
B2m and B2u raise M2 from 0.60 to 0.65 at the cost of extra edge compute or stricter upload utility; full 3-seed sweeps are in the online supplement.
